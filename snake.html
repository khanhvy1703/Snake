How to play
×

 The users control the direction of the snake's head by using the arrows key and make the snake eat the food . The users can't stop the snake while the game is in process.
 The snake's tail will grow up and be longer every time it eats the food. The score will be increased by 1.
 However, there is a poison food on the map. If the snake eats the poison food, it will lose one tail and the score will be decreased by 1.
 The game will be over if the snake eats itself or hits the wall.
